Grilled Chicken & Avocado Salad

A satisfying, protein-packed salad with grilled chicken, creamy avocado, and crisp vegetables.

🕒 20 min 🔥 450 kcal
🍗 40g Protein 🍞 25g Carbs 🥬 22g Fat

Preparation Method

  • Gather all the ingredients for Grilled Chicken & Avocado Salad
  • Wash and prepare the vegetables (lettuce, tomatoes, avocado, onion).
  • Preheat the grill or grill pan over medium heat.
  • Season or marinate the chicken breast with olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, salt, and pepper
  • Grill the chicken for about 6–7 minutes on each side until fully cooked
  • While the chicken cooks, slice the avocado, chop the tomatoes, and thinly slice the onion
  • In a bowl, layer the greens, avocado, tomato, and onion.
  • Slice the grilled chicken and add it on top of the salad .
  • Drizzle with a simple dressing (olive oil + lemon juice + salt & pepper).
  • Plate the Grilled Chicken & Avocado Salad attractively and serve!
